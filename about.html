A short
biography.

IT Product Architect at Ericsson, with a background across computer science, cloud platforms, AI prototypes, and product-facing transformation work.

Stockholm-based IT Product Architect working where architecture, product direction, and enterprise cloud delivery meet.

My work sits close to roadmap decisions, governance, and cross-organisational initiatives: translating strategic direction into clearer product and engineering choices. I am especially drawn to the space between business intent and technical execution, where good architecture makes decisions easier rather than heavier.

I came into the role through Ericsson's IT Trainee Program, rotating through data science, cloud engineering, and business operations. In those rotations I worked with GenAI prototypes, RAG and text-to-SQL agents, Azure resource management, PowerShell and Bicep, FinOps and sustainability dashboards, and IT governance evaluation. Alongside architectural responsibilities, I still enjoy building practical automation with RPA and the Microsoft Power Platform.

Before KTH, I spent several school years in music, art, and design tracks, and joined The Junior Academy during high school. That mix of technical problem-solving, visual thinking, and collaborative work still shapes how I approach systems, communication, and decision-making.

The less formal notes live in Miscellany: books, food, choir, plants, trails, and other things that sit just outside the CV.
