Participant and activity organizer, helping create moments of companionship and community for elderly residents.

Activity organizer

SEP 2017 — SEP 2018 · PART-TIME

Through Ung Omsorg, I took part in activities at nursing homes in Stockholm County, supporting initiatives designed to bring joy, companionship, and a stronger sense of community to elderly residents.

My role combined participation with practical activity organizing: showing up consistently, creating a welcoming atmosphere, and helping residents feel seen through shared social moments.

Community work

The experience gave me an early appreciation for service, reliability, and human-centered organization: small activities can make a meaningful difference when they are planned with care.
